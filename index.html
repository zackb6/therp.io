Frequently Asked Questions

Is this therapy?

No. Therpf is a tool for self-insight, not a replacement for therapy. A therapist guides a session and dictates the pace. With Therpf, you are in complete control. You decide when to be honest with yourself and how deep to go, making it a powerful tool for personal discovery, but distinct from clinical therapy.

How is my privacy protected?

We take your privacy extremely seriously. All conversations are encrypted and anonymized. We do not sell your data. You can read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

What techniques is the AI built on?

Our AI is built on the principles of evidence-based psychological methods, primarily Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT). It helps you identify, challenge, and reframe negative thought patterns in a structured way to achieve insight.
