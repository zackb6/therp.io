Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. If you need more help, feel free to contact us.

Is Therpf.ai a replacement for human therapy?

Therpf.ai is designed to be a tool for mental wellness and a companion for your thoughts. It is not a replacement for diagnosis or treatment by a human therapist, especially for severe mental health conditions. It can be a great first step or a supplement to traditional therapy.

How is my privacy protected?

We take your privacy extremely seriously. All conversations are encrypted and anonymized. We do not sell your data. You can read our full Privacy Policy for more details.

What techniques does the AI use?

Our AI is built on the principles of evidence-based therapeutic methods, primarily Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT). It helps you identify, challenge, and reframe negative thought patterns in a structured way.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your monthly or annual subscription at any time through your account settings. You will retain access until the end of your current billing period.
